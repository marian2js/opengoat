## `MARKET.md` structure
Use this structure:

# MARKET

## ICP hypotheses
- Who is most likely to buy this product
- Company stage / size
- Industry / function
- Signals supporting this

## Personas
- Economic buyer
- End user
- Influencer / champion
- Likely pains and desired outcomes

## Main customer pains
- Problems the product seems to solve
- Urgency triggers
- Why customers would look for this now

## Competitor landscape
### Direct competitors
- Name
- Short note on overlap

### Indirect competitors / alternatives
- Agencies
- Internal workflows
- Generic tools
- “Do nothing” option

## Category language
- Terms this market uses
- Terms competitors use
- Terms that appear overused / generic

## Differentiation hypotheses
- Possible wedge
- What seems unique
- Where the company may be stronger / weaker

## Buying objections and friction
- Likely reasons someone would hesitate
- Pricing / trust / complexity / switching costs

## Market opportunities
- Underserved angles
- Messaging gaps
- Segment opportunities

## Open questions
- Important uncertainties to resolve later
