# Introducing OpenGoat: Scalable AI Agent Organizations

In a world where AI is transforming how we work, OpenGoat emerges as a platform to build hierarchical organizations of AI agents that turn vague goals into concrete, reliable outcomes.

## Our Vision
OpenGoat exists to enable trustworthy, efficient delegation in AI-driven teams. We prioritize clarity, ownership, and minimal waste.

## Key Features
- **Agent Hierarchy**: Organize agents like a real company structure.
- **Task Tracking**: Assign and monitor tasks with full visibility.
- **Tool Integration**: Connect to external APIs and tools seamlessly.
- **Developer SDK**: Customize and extend with ease (coming soon).

See our [product roadmap](https://github.com/marian2js/opengoat/blob/main/docs/product-roadmap.md) for more.
